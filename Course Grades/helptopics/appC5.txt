The file menu on the menu bar provides minor functionality for dealing with the particular window. You can save your grades just like the Update and Save Grades button, forcefully reopen the launch menu, and exit the application. The latter 2 options will either let you to choose and save or just perform whatever action needed. If unsaved data is detected when choosing not to save, you will be prompted with a confirmation prompt where you can choose to save, not save, or cancel the operation requested.
